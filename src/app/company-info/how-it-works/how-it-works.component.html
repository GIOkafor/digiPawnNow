How It Works

Overview Of The Order Process
  1. Create your account
  2. Use our search bar to find your item
  3. Select your item and its condition
  4. Select how you want to be paid (US Dollars, Bitcoin, Ethereum or Litecoin)
  5. Place your order
  6. Within 24 hours of placing your order, you will receive an email from us with a free FedEx Ground shipping label
  7. Pack your item and drop it off at a FedEx location
Items That We Take
  • Cell phones, headphones, Apple watches, iPods, iPads, Kindles, MacBooks, iMacs and game consoles that can found in our search bar/database
Payment Process
  • After we receive your item(s), we will inspect the item(s) and then, issue a payment to you within two business days of when we physically receive your item(s)
